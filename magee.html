Helps triage cases for molecular testing using equation results and mitotic activity score.

Application Guidelines
  • Do Not Send: All scores <18, or 18-25 with mitosis score 1
  • Send for Testing: Any score >25 or 18-25 with mitosis score ≥2

Development History
  • Derived from 800+ cases (2004-2009)
  • Validated on 200+ independent cases
  • Updated per TAILORx trial criteria

Category IHC FISH
Negative 0/1+ or 2+ <4 copies/cell
Equivocal 2+ 4-5.9 copies/cell
Positive 3+ ≥6 copies/cell

Calculated as: (0×% negative) + (1×% weak) + (2×% moderate) + (3×% strong)

Example:
10% negative → 0×10 = 0
30% weak → 1×30 = 30
40% moderate → 2×40 = 80
20% strong → 3×20 = 60
Total H-Score = 0 + 30 + 80 + 60 = 170
