Frequently Used Calculators

These calculators are commonly used by pathologists for breast cancer prognostication and reporting.

Magee Equations Calculator

Estimate Oncotype DX recurrence score using pathology data. Contains all information from the UPMC Magee Equations webpage.

Open Calculator
H-score Calculator

Calculates the semi-quantitative immunohistochemical H-score for ER, PR, or other stains. The H-score is the sum of the percent staining multiplied by intensity (0–3). Range: 0–300.

Open Calculator
Nottingham Prognostic Index Calculator

Calculates the Nottingham Prognostic Index (NPI) for breast cancer using tumor size, lymph node status, and grade.

Open Calculator
Pre-operative Endocrine Prognostic Index (PEPI score) Calculator

Calculates PEPI score for those who received neoadjuvant endocrine therapy alone, based on pT stage, pN stage, Ki67 level, and ER status.

Open Calculator
Residual Cancer Burden Calculator

Tool for residual cancer burden assessment after neoadjuvant therapy.
