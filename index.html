Breast Pathology Calculators

This website provides a suite of breast cancer risk and pathology calculators intended for use by healthcare professionals.

Calculators Frequently Used by Pathologists
Magee Equations Calculator

Estimate Oncotype DX recurrence score using pathology data.

Open Calculator
H-score Calculator

Calculates the semi-quantitative immunohistochemical H-score for ER, PR, or other stains. The H-score is the sum of the percent staining multiplied by intensity.

Open Calculator
Nottingham Prognostic Index Calculator

Calculates the Nottingham Prognostic Index (NPI) for breast cancer using tumor size, lymph node status, and grade.

Open Calculator
Pre-operative Endocrine Prognostic Index (PEPI score) Calculator

Calculates PEPI score for those who received neoadjuvant endocrine therapy alone, based on pT stage, pN stage, Ki67 level, and ER status.

Open Calculator
Residual Cancer Burden Calculator

Tool for residual cancer burden assessment after neoadjuvant therapy.
