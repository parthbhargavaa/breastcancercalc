Breast Cancer Calculator

A one-stop platform for evidence-based, pathology-informed breast cancer decision support tools, designed to empower patients and professionals alike.

Magee Equations Calculator

Estimates recurrence risk scores based on standard pathology metrics (ER/PR, HER2, Nottingham score, Ki-67, tumor size). Intended to approximate Oncotype DX.

H-Score Calculator

Semi-quantitative scoring system based on staining intensity and percentage for IHC markers like ER and PR.

Nottingham Prognostic Index

Combines tumor size, nodal status, and grade to estimate 10-year survival. Based on long-term prognostic studies.

PEPI Score Calculator

Used for patients post-endocrine therapy to stratify relapse risk based on tumor stage, nodal status, Ki-67, and ER.
