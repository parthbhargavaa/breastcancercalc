Tumor Volume Reduction Calculator

Estimates tumor size and volume reduction in the breast after neoadjuvant therapy using the Magee method. This tool calculates the percentage reduction based on pre-therapy tumor size and the revised tumor size corrected for cellularity after treatment.

Tumor Volume Reduction Percentage: -%

This calculator estimates tumor volume reduction in breast cancer patients after neoadjuvant therapy using the Magee method. It combines the measured post-therapy tumor bed dimension corrected for tumor cellularity relative to the pre-therapy biopsy to estimate residual tumor volume. The tumor volume reduction percentage is calculated as the difference between pre-therapy and revised tumor size, normalized by the pre-therapy tumor size.

Formula:
Revised tumor size = Post-therapy tumor bed dimension × (Tumor cellularity ÷ 100)
Tumor volume reduction (%) = ((Pre-therapy size - Revised tumor size) ÷ Pre-therapy size) × 100

This method helps in assessing response to neoadjuvant therapy, aiding treatment planning and prognosis.
